Privacy Policy

CH4YN / KLANGWERK

Last updated: February 2026

Who We Are

CH4YN is a music project by KLANGWERK. When you sign up for our mailing list, we collect limited personal information to send you updates about new music releases, events, and related content.

What We Collect

  • Email address — to send you email updates
  • First name (optional) — to personalize our emails

We do not collect payment information, browsing data, cookies, or any other personal data through our mailing list.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Send you email updates about new CH4YN music releases
  • Notify you about upcoming events or shows
  • Share occasional behind-the-scenes content

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Email Service Provider

We use SendGrid (by Twilio) to manage and send our emails. SendGrid processes your email address and name on our behalf. Their privacy policy is available at twilio.com/legal/privacy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of every email
  • Request deletion of your data by emailing us
  • Access the data we hold about you by emailing us

If you are in the EU, you have additional rights under the GDPR, including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Data Retention

We retain your email and name for as long as you remain subscribed. When you unsubscribe, your data is removed from our active mailing list. Residual data may persist in backups for up to 30 days.
